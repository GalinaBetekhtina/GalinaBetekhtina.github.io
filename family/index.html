"Guten Tag!"

Dear landlord or property manager,

My name is Andrei and along with my family we are looking for an apartment.

Let me introduce our family...

Our family

Our family consist of 3 persons and our small 10-year-old dog. We arrived to Bamberg because of a Blue Card according to my work contract with Antolin Deutschland GmbH. We moved to Bamberg from Finland where we have lived since 2022. We are originally from Russia, where we have two our own apartments. Therefore, we clearly understand how is important take good care of the housing allocated to us for renting.
We believe, that after this introducing of our family you will be sure that you can entrust us with renting the apartment.

Andrei

I am 34 years old and I am electronics engineer. I have a Master’s degree in Electronic Components and Systems and more than 10-year-experience in software and hardware development of embedded system. I worked in Ready for Sky (Saint-Petersburg, Russia), where I started as hardware design engineer to develop smart devices controlled by smartphone. After a while I continued work there as a software engineer and became a team leader. In 2022 I received a job offer in Ampliconyx (Tampere, Finland). My family and I moved to Finland, where I worked as a Research and Development engineer. Now, I am a hardware design engineer in Antolin Deutschland GmbH.
I don't have any bad habits and I prefer to lead an active lifestyle. I like a snowboarding, rides on a bicycle, a table tennis, play football and etc. Also, I like quiet family evenings to watch movies or playing board games.

Galina

My wife is 36 years old. She also has a Master’s degree in Electronic Components and Systems and 10-year-experience in microwave electronic system. She started learning a front-end development on her own several years ago. For example, this web-site it is one of her projects. She is planning to become a front-end developer after a German language integration course. Traveling and meeting new people always brings her joy and inspiration. She enjoys outdoor activities, especially skiing, and often spends time playing football with our son.

Ilia

Let me introduce our five-year-old son. Ilya is a true athlete at heart and he spreads cheerfulness and energy. He can’t imagine his life without football. He looks forward to every game to show his skills on the field. In winter, Ilya turns into a brave skier, and he boldly come down from the snow-covered slopes. In the summer, he never tires of conquering the tracks on his bicycle. Ilya has an insatiable desire to learn new things. He is always on the lookout for interesting facts and knowledge. Lego is not just toy for him. It is an opportunity to create entire worlds, where each block is a step towards a new discovery.

Yuji

Yuji it is our dear West Highland White Terrier. He has been delighting us with his presence for 10 years. From the first days, Yuji proved himself as a great four-legged friend with good manners, thanks to early training with a dog handler. Yuji has not damaged any things in our house, during his long life. Today, Yuji prefers a quiet lifestyle, spending time in his favorite dog bed where he can relax and enjoy the world around him. He appreciates every minute spent next to us, especially when we give him affection and attention.

Conclusion

Moving to Germany for my family and me it is not just a changing place of residence. My wife and I started thinking about moving in 2018. It was a balanced and meaningful decision. There is the huge labor market, offices of all international companies, climate is better than in Saint-Petersburg, and some sentimental reason. I was born in Karl-Marx-Stadt because my father was a military man. And all of my childhood I heard positive memories about Germany from my parents. Also, I have distant relatives of mine and several friends in Germany. These factors played a key role in our decision.
